Can I solve the tasks directly on Edgar and compile them there?
No, you cannot solve the tasks directly on Edgar and compile them there. The Edgar system is used for automatic assessment of tests of theoretical and practical knowledge from the subject Introduction into programming. In this course, you will encounter tasks of the programming type, where is for default problem, it is necessary to write a programming solution in the programming language C. Tests contain programming tasks that are solved and tested in the Notepad(++) or development editor. You need to copy-paste your solution from the text field of the task into the Edgar system.
